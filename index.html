Profile

About Me

I am a dedicated researcher specializing in advanced micro-assembly technologies and heterogeneous integration systems. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions for Micro-LED displays and next-generation semiconductor packaging.

With expertise in fluidic self-assembly, photolithography processes, and low-temperature bonding techniques, I strive to push the boundaries of what's possible in micro-scale manufacturing and integration.

My research aims to bridge the gap between laboratory innovations and scalable industrial applications, contributing to the advancement of display technologies and semiconductor integration platforms.

Research Focus

Micro-LED Smart Assembly Systems

Developing cutting-edge assembly and integration technologies for next-generation Micro-LED displays and heterogeneous semiconductor systems through innovative fluidic self-assembly and advanced packaging techniques.

Core Concept

Leveraging fluidic self-assembly (FSA) combined with advanced photolithography and bonding technologies to achieve precise, high-throughput integration of micro-scale components for displays and semiconductor systems. This approach enables scalable manufacturing of complex heterogeneous systems with unprecedented precision and efficiency.

Key Technologies

Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA)

  • High-precision component placement using fluid dynamics
  • Parallel assembly of thousands of micro-components
  • Shape-matched receptor-chip integration
  • Optimized assembly efficiency and yield

SU-8/OmniCoat Photolithography

  • Advanced receptor pattern fabrication
  • High aspect ratio microstructure creation
  • Sacrificial layer processing
  • Precise dimensional control

Low-Temperature Alloy Bonding

  • Bi-Sn and In-Sn alloy systems
  • Temperature-controlled phase transitions
  • Reliable electrical and mechanical connections
  • Thermal stress minimization

Process Optimization

  • Flow rate and assembly parameter tuning
  • Yield enhancement strategies
  • Defect reduction methodologies
  • Scalability assessment and improvement
