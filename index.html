Park Jaesung is a graduate student specializing in Micro-LED assembly technologies and advanced packaging processes. His research focuses on fluidic self-assembly (FSA), photoresist-assisted heterogeneous particle alignment, and low-temperature interconnect solutions. He is dedicated to advancing next-generation display technologies and semiconductor integration platforms through innovative micro-assembly techniques and process optimization.

Research

Micro-LED Smart Assembly Systems

Advanced Process Engineering for Next-Generation Displays

Core Concept

  • We assemble micro-scale LED chips using photoresist-assisted fluidic self-assembly.
  • Low-temperature, selective interconnects enable precise placement without unintended soldering.

Key Technologies

Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA)

Innovative micro-chip assembly technique using fluid dynamics for precise positioning and high-throughput parallel integration.

SU-8 / OmniCoat Engineering

Sacrificial layer design for controlled assembly yield and structural integrity with precise dimensional control.
